Course Content

2 days of intensive training with focused and structured learning plan to ensure that you have both theoretical understanding and skills to practically apply these learning to real time projects.

WHAT WILL YOU LEARN ?

  • Website Creation through WORDPRESS
  • Search Engine Marketing (SEM)
  • Social Media Marketing
  • Social Media Management
  • Search Engine Optimisation (On page SEO, Off page SEO)
  • Google Analytics
  • Mobile App Creation
Modules

Module 1: Website Creation through WORDPRESS

  • Build a functional, beautiful, responsive website.
  • Familiarise with Wordpress Dashboard: Navigation, Functions and Features.
  • Understand categories of Posts and importance of right Tagging.
  • Build customised menus and navigation systems
  • Understand different plugins & themes: Installation, Features and Usability
  • Build customised menus and navigation systems
  • Protection tips against hackers and spammers

Module 2: Search Engine Marketing (SEM)

  • Online Advertising Fundamentals.
  • Increasing traffic to your website.
  • Optimize desktop websites for mobile phones.
  • Learn the basics of Pay-Pay-Click (PPC) marketing.
  • Basic video marketing campaigns
  • Tricks and techniques for great ROI, and run campaigns for online sales, lead generation or brand building
  • Prepare you for the Google Adword Certification exams

Module 3: Social Media Marketing

  • Understand how Social Media Marketing works and possibilities
  • Create high quality and optimised paid ads on different Social Media platforms.
  • Instagram and Facebook Marketing and how to monetize these social platforms.

Module 4: Social Media Management

  • Fundamentals of Youtube, Facebook, Google+, Twitter, Instagram and blogging.
  • Use advanced features of the most popular social networks
  • Understand the wide scope of different social media channels
  • Recognise which social media platforms are best for each client.
  • The best tools to use as a social media manager (with step-by-step instructions)
  • Conducting social media audits and creating monthly reports

Module 5: Search Engine Optimisation (On page SEO, Off page SEO)

  • Different SEO factors and their impact on Google website ranking
  • Keywords and their different types
  • Keyword research, analysis and validation
  • SEO to generate organic and targeted traffic from search engines
  • Competitor's SEO research
  • Optimizing website for Google, Bing & Yandex search engines
  • Free SEO tools available; which ones to use and how to use them
  • Creating powerful back links and identify bad backlinks to your site
  • Tips to protect against Negative SEO attacks
  • Improve website loading speed and bounce rate

Module 6: Google Analytics

  • Set Up Google Analytics to Collect Actionable Data.
  • Navigating the Interface.
  • Advanced Table Filtering.
  • Creating Segments.
  • Multiple Goals And Goal Types.
  • Track the Success of Your Marketing with Campaign Tagging
  • Document Insights and Make Data Driven Decisions to Grow.
  • Analytics Intelligence.
  • Prepare you for the Google Analytics Certification exams

Employees | Housewives | Business Owners | Self-employed | Retired | Anyone who intends to start a career in the Digital Marketing field

Age, Experience, and Qualification are NOT the criteria. No technical or coding experience required.
